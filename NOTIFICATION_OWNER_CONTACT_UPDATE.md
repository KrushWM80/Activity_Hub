### **What You Need To Do**

#### **Option 1: Update in Intake Hub (Recommended)**
If your project originated in Intake Hub, update the organizational contacts there:

1. Go to **[Intake Hub Projects](https://hoops.wal-mart.com/intake-hub/projects)**
2. Open your project
3. Navigate to **Contacts** section
4. Update the following fields:
   - **Project Director** (Your direct manager)
   - **Project Sr. Director** (Director's manager)
   - **VP** (Sr. Director's manager)
5. Click **Save**

**Why?** Intake Hub is the source of truth. Changes sync automatically to Activity Hub.

---

#### **Option 2: Update Directly in Activity Hub (Projects-Only)**
If your project was manually created in Activity Hub:

1. Open your project in **Activity Hub**
2. Locate the **Leadership** section
3. Enter or edit:
   - **Director:** (Your direct supervisor's name)
   - **Sr. Director:** (Their manager's name)
4. Click **Save & Sync**

**Note:** Changes are stored locally and do not affect Intake Hub.
