# Walmart Store Associate Image Prompts Guide

**Purpose**: Guidelines for creating AI-generated images of Walmart associates that are professional, brand-appropriate, and suitable for corporate use.

**Date Created**: January 14, 2026

---

## ✅ Successful Prompt Example

**Prompt Used**:
```
A professional photo of a Walmart store associate in a blue vest and khaki pants 
standing in a retail aisle, looking at a smartphone in their hands with a focused, 
productive expression. The aisle has neatly organized shelves with products on 
both sides. Bright, clean store lighting. The associate appears engaged and 
professional, using the phone as a work tool. Modern retail environment. 
Photorealistic style, high quality, corporate photography aesthetic. Shot from 
a slight angle showing both the associate and the store aisle context. Natural 
workplace scene.
```

**Result**: Professional, brand-appropriate image with associate in official uniform using smartphone in store setting.

---

## 🎯 Key Elements to Include

### 1. **Uniform Details**
- ✅ **Blue vest** with Walmart spark logo
- ✅ **Name badge** visible
- ✅ **Khaki/tan pants** or **dark pants**
- ✅ **Gray or light blue polo shirt** underneath vest
- ✅ Professional appearance

### 2. **Setting & Environment**
- ✅ **Store aisle** with shelves
- ✅ **Bright, clean lighting** (typical retail fluorescent)
- ✅ **Organized products** on shelves
- ✅ **Wide aisles** with clear sightlines
- ✅ Modern, well-maintained store appearance

### 3. **Body Language & Expression**
- ✅ **Focused and engaged**
- ✅ **Professional demeanor**
- ✅ **Confident posture**
- ✅ **Natural workplace behavior**
- ✅ Productive, not distracted

### 4. **Technology Usage**
- ✅ **Smartphone** as work tool
- ✅ **Natural hand position** (both hands holding phone)
- ✅ Looking at screen with purpose
- ✅ Professional use context (not casual/social)

### 5. **Photography Style**
- ✅ **Photorealistic** quality
- ✅ **Corporate photography** aesthetic
- ✅ **High resolution** appearance
- ✅ **Natural colors** (not oversaturated)
- ✅ Professional composition

## ⚠️ Things to Avoid

### ❌ Uniform Issues
- Wrong color vest (not blue)
- Missing spark logo
- Casual clothing instead of uniform
- Unprofessional appearance

### ❌ Setting Issues
- Messy or disorganized shelves
- Dark or dim lighting
- Non-retail environments
- Cluttered backgrounds

### ❌ Behavior Issues
- Distracted or unfocused expressions
- Casual/social phone use (scrolling, selfies)
- Unprofessional posture or body language
- Activities unrelated to work

### ❌ Style Issues
- Cartoon or illustrated style (unless specifically needed)
- Unrealistic or exaggerated features
- Poor quality or blurry
- Overly staged or artificial poses

## ✨ Best Practices

1. **Be Specific**: Detail uniform, setting, and behavior clearly
2. **Emphasize Professionalism**: Use terms like "corporate," "professional," "work tool"
3. **Request Photorealism**: Specify "photorealistic style" and "high quality"
4. **Natural Context**: Ask for "natural workplace scene" to avoid overly staged looks
5. **Iterate if Needed**: Refine prompt based on results
