Quick and Easy Tool to Determine Your Platform's Development Needs

Welcome to Platform Assessment

This tool helps you understand the full scope of work needed for each Store Support Platform. Simply answer a few simple questions, and we'll provide a comprehensive assessment of requirements, complexity, and estimated costs.

How It Works:

  1. Select - Choose whether you're assessing an existing platform or designing new one
  2. Answer - Simple questions about your platform's needs (no technical knowledge required)
  3. Review - Get a detailed assessment with complexity levels and cost estimates
  4. Export - Save results for team discussion and planning

  • Basic: Everyone has identical access (e.g., dashboard view only)
  • Standard: Different permission levels by role (Admin creates, Manager approves, Viewer reads)
  • Advanced: Custom logic like "Sales staff only see their region" or "Managers see their team"
