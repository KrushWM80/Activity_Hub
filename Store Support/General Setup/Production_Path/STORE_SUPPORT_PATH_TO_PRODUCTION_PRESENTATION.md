## Presentation Tips for Delivery

**Opening (Slides 1-4):**
- Lead with the two-part structure immediately
- Emphasize this is about capability PLUS product
- Set up the "why separate?" question early

**Middle (Slides 5-14):**
- Deep dive each part independently
- Use visuals to show ROI for each
- Connect the dots between infrastructure and product value

**Closing (Slides 15-23):**
- Bring it back together for combined view
- Address risks head-on
- Strong call to action with clear next steps

**Q&A Strategy:**
- Anticipate questions about timeline, costs, and risks
- Have appendix slides ready for technical deep-dives
- Stay focused on business value, not just technology

**Key Messages to Reinforce:**
1. "Two investments, one strategic decision"
2. "Path to Production enables unlimited products"
3. "Activity Hub proves the value immediately"
4. "5,420% ROI makes this a no-brainer"
5. "Every month of delay costs $262K"
