# 📊 Step 1: Data Quality Champion (DQC) Assignment

**Timeline:** 1-3 days  
**Owner:** Your Pillar's Data Governance Lead  
**Prerequisites:** Step 0: Clipper Setup (Product ID created)

---

## 🎯 Overview

The Data Quality Champion (DQC) is your gateway to the entire production onboarding process. **Only a DQC can submit new Product Team requests in Team Rosters**, which is Step 2 of the Production Path.

Without a DQC, you cannot proceed with the production onboarding process.

**Note:** You should complete Step 0 (Clipper Setup) BEFORE contacting your DQC to ensure your Product ID is created.

---

## ✅ What You Need to Do

### For Store Support / Walmart US Pillar Products:

**Your DQC:** Kevin Tadda  
**Business Group:** Walmart US (Business Groups - Walmart US pillar)

1. [ ] Contact Kevin Tadda to inform him you'll need Team Rosters Product ID request (Step 2)
2. [ ] Coordinate with Kevin on timeline for your product registration
3. [ ] Move to Step 2: Team Rosters

### Option 1: You Already Have a DQC (Other Pillars)
1. [ ] Find your DQC in the [DQC Network List](https://walmart.sharepoint.com/sites/dqc-network)
2. [ ] Document their contact information
3. [ ] Reach out to inform them you'll need Team Rosters Product ID request (Step 2)
4. [ ] Move to Step 2: Team Rosters

### Option 2: You Don't Have a DQC (Other Pillars)
1. [ ] Identify your pillar's data governance lead
2. [ ] Contact them via email or Teams
3. [ ] Request DQC assignment for your team/product
4. [ ] Await assignment confirmation (typically 1-3 days)
5. [ ] Document new DQC contact information
6. [ ] Move to Step 2: Team Rosters

---

## 📋 Required Information

### To Find Your DQC:
- **Resource:** [DQC Network List](https://walmart.sharepoint.com/sites/dqc-network)
- **Search by:** Your product team, pillar, or business unit
- **Verify:** Confirm the DQC is still active and available

### To Request DQC Assignment:
- **Your team/product name**
- **Your pillar** (e.g., Tech, Merchandising, Supply Chain, etc.)
- **Your manager or director's name**
- **Business justification** (brief explanation of your product/project)

---

## 🔑 Why DQC Matters

### DQC Responsibilities:
- ✅ Submit Product Team requests in Team Rosters
- ✅ Ensure data quality standards are met
- ✅ Act as governance liaison for data-related decisions
- ✅ Approve data classification and usage patterns

### What DQC is NOT:
- ❌ Not a blocker - they're an enabler
- ❌ Not responsible for technical implementation
- ❌ Not the application owner or technical owner

## ⚠️ Common Issues & Solutions

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Can't find DQC in network list | Contact your pillar's data governance lead for assignment |
| DQC is no longer with team | Request new DQC assignment through data governance |
| DQC is unavailable/on leave | Request temporary DQC coverage through data governance |
| Multiple teams share a DQC | Coordinate with DQC on timing for your request |
