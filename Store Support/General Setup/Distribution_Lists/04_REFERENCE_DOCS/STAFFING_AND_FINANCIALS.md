## COST-BENEFIT ANALYSIS

### **Costs (3-Year Total: $180M - $204M)**

**Breakdown:**
- **People (FTE + Contractor):** $160M - $180M (85-90% of total)
- **Infrastructure & Platform:** $7M - $9M (4-5%)
- **Software Licenses:** $2.3M - $3M (1-2%)
- **Training & Enablement:** $1M - $1.2M (0.5-1%)

---

### **Benefits (Estimated 3-Year Value)**

#### **Quantifiable Benefits**

1. **Reduced Manual Reporting Effort**
   - **Current State:** 500+ analysts spend 30% of time on manual reporting
   - **Future State:** Automated via Activity Hub, OMP, MyWalmart
   - **Value:** 150 FTE-years × $100K = **$15M over 3 years**

2. **Decommissioned Legacy Tools**
   - **Tableau/PowerBI licenses:** $2M/year savings (years 2-3) = **$4M**
   - **Legacy infrastructure:** $1M/year savings (years 2-3) = **$2M**
   - **Total:** **$6M**

3. **Improved Decision Speed**
   - **Metric:** Reduce time-to-insight from 3 days → 30 minutes
   - **Value:** 10% improvement in operational efficiency for 1,000 managers
   - **Estimated value:** $5K/manager/year = **$15M over 3 years**

4. **Store Operations Efficiency**
   - **OMP + MyWalmart:** Better labor scheduling, execution tracking
   - **Metric:** 1% labor efficiency improvement across 4,700 stores
   - **Value:** 1% of $50M labor cost/store = **$500K/store × 4,700 = $2.3B**
   - **Conservative attribution (10%):** **$230M over 3 years**

5. **Data Science ROI**
   - **Predictive models:** Demand forecasting, shrink reduction, labor optimization
   - **Conservative estimate:** 5 high-impact models at $5M value each
   - **Total:** **$25M over 3 years**

#### **Total Quantifiable Benefits: $291M over 3 years**

---

### **Intangible Benefits**

- **Consistent Metrics:** Apples-to-apples comparisons across business
- **Executive Confidence:** Trusted data for decision-making
- **Employee Satisfaction:** Modern tools vs legacy frustration
- **Scalability:** Platform supports future growth without linear cost increase
- **Competitive Advantage:** Data-driven culture

## KEY TAKEAWAYS

### **Staffing Philosophy**

1. **Invest in Platform First** — Data Platform and Activity Hub are force multipliers
2. **Product Teams Own Outcomes** — Not just engineers; PMs, analysts, designers included
3. **Capabilities Are Centralized** — Data Science and Analytics Engineering serve all
4. **Governance Is Lightweight** — Small team, big leverage through platforms
5. **Contractor Flex** — Use contractors for migration waves, FTEs for ownership

### **Financial Philosophy**

1. **$68M-75M Steady State Is Justified** — ROI is 43-62% over 3 years
2. **Store Efficiency Is Key** — Even small % gains in 4,700 stores = massive value
3. **Legacy Costs Must Be Tracked** — Show savings as Tableau/PowerBI decommissioned
4. **Phase Funding** — Don't commit to full 350 FTEs upfront; prove value incrementally

### **Hiring Philosophy**

1. **VPs First, Then Senior Directors** — Leadership drives clarity
2. **Hire for Future Skills** — React, Python, DBT, ML over legacy Tableau
3. **Upskill Existing Team** — Don't abandon people who know the business
4. **Diversity in Experience** — Mix of consultants (pace) and internal (context)
