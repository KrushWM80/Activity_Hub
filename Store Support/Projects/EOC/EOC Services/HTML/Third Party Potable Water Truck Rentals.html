Third Party Potable Water Truck Rentals

Emergency Water Supply Services

EMERGENCY SERVICE: This service provides mobile potable water supply during emergencies when regular water services are disrupted or contaminated.

Service Overview

Third Party Potable Water Truck Rentals provide essential clean drinking water during emergency situations, natural disasters, and infrastructure failures. These mobile water supply units help maintain public health by ensuring access to safe drinking water for affected communities and emergency response personnel.

Services Provided

  • Mobile potable water delivery trucks
  • Water storage and distribution systems
  • Water quality testing and certification
  • 24/7 emergency water supply service
  • Distribution equipment (hoses, connectors)
  • Water purification and treatment capabilities

Activation Process

Note: Please refer to the original Word document for specific activation procedures, contact information, and operational details.

  1. Contact EOC for emergency water service activation
  2. Specify location and estimated water volume needed
  3. Provide access information and delivery schedule
  4. Coordinate distribution points and methods
  5. Establish refill and resupply schedule

Resource Requirements

  • Vehicle access for water truck delivery
  • Designated water distribution area
  • Storage containers or distribution system
  • Personnel for distribution management
  • Traffic control for delivery operations

Water Supply Specifications

  • FDA-approved potable water trucks
  • Water quality meets EPA standards
  • Various truck capacities available
  • Sanitized delivery systems
  • Temperature-controlled storage options
  • Certified water testing documentation

Distribution Methods

  • Direct truck-to-container filling
  • Temporary water storage tanks
  • Individual bottled water distribution
  • Bulk water dispensing stations
  • Connection to temporary water systems
