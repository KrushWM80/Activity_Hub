Third Party Portable Restroom Rentals

Emergency Sanitation Services Provider

EMERGENCY SERVICE: This service provides portable restroom facilities during emergencies when regular sanitation services are disrupted or unavailable.

Service Overview

Third Party Portable Restroom Rentals provide essential sanitation facilities during emergency situations, disaster response operations, and temporary relief efforts. These units help maintain public health and sanitation standards for affected communities, emergency personnel, and relief workers.

Services Provided

  • Standard portable restroom units
  • ADA-compliant accessible units
  • Hand washing stations
  • Emergency waste management
  • Regular maintenance and cleaning services
  • Waste pump-out and sanitization

Activation Process

Note: Please refer to the original Word document for specific activation procedures, contact information, and operational details.

  1. Contact EOC for service activation request
  2. Specify number of units needed and location
  3. Provide duration of service requirement
  4. Coordinate delivery schedule and placement
  5. Arrange maintenance and service schedule

Resource Requirements

  • Level ground for unit placement
  • Vehicle access for delivery and service
  • Designated placement areas
  • Security considerations if needed
  • Proper spacing for accessibility compliance

Unit Types Available

  • Standard portable restrooms
  • ADA-compliant accessible units
  • Enhanced comfort units with sinks
  • Family/companion care units
  • Hand washing stations (standalone)
  • High-capacity units for large groups

Service Schedule

  • Regular cleaning and maintenance
  • Waste removal and sanitization
  • Supply restocking (toilet paper, sanitizer)
  • 24/7 emergency service availability
  • Damage repair and unit replacement
