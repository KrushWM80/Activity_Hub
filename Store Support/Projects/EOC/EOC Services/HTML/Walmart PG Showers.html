Walmart PG Showers

Emergency Mobile Shower Services

EMERGENCY SERVICE: This service provides emergency mobile shower facilities through Walmart and P&G partnership during disasters and emergencies.

Service Overview

Walmart PG Showers is a collaborative emergency service between Walmart and Procter & Gamble that provides mobile shower facilities during natural disasters and crisis situations. This service helps maintain hygiene and dignity for disaster victims, emergency responders, and relief workers.

Services Provided

  • Mobile shower unit deployment
  • Hot water shower facilities
  • Privacy and safety features
  • P&G hygiene product supplies
  • Professional operation and maintenance
  • Community outreach coordination

Activation Process

Note: Please refer to the original Word document for specific activation procedures, contact information, and operational details.

  1. Contact EOC for Walmart PG Showers activation
  2. Assess community shower facility needs
  3. Coordinate with Walmart emergency response team
  4. Identify deployment location and requirements
  5. Schedule mobile unit delivery and setup

Resource Requirements

  • Accessible location for mobile shower units
  • Electrical power connections
  • Fresh water supply access
  • Waste water disposal capability
  • Propane supply for hot water heating
  • Safe parking and access area

Partnership Features

  • Walmart logistics and deployment capability
  • P&G hygiene products and supplies
  • Corporate disaster relief experience
  • Professional maintenance and operation
  • Community-focused service approach
  • Coordinated emergency response network

Facility Specifications

  • Individual shower stalls with privacy
  • Hot water on-demand systems
  • Climate-controlled environment
  • Non-slip safety flooring
  • Towels and basic hygiene supplies
  • ADA-compliant accessibility options
