Walmart Fleet Cook Team

Emergency Mobile Feeding Operations

EMERGENCY SERVICE: This service provides emergency mobile kitchen and feeding operations during disasters and large-scale emergencies.

Service Overview

Walmart Fleet Cook Team provides emergency mobile kitchen services during natural disasters and crisis situations. This service deploys mobile cooking units and professional kitchen staff to provide hot meals for affected communities, emergency responders, and relief workers during extended emergency operations.

Services Provided

  • Mobile kitchen and cooking units
  • Professional kitchen staff and cooks
  • Large-scale meal preparation capability
  • Hot food service and distribution
  • Food safety and sanitation compliance
  • Nutritious meal planning and preparation

Activation Process

Note: Please refer to the original Word document for specific activation procedures, contact information, and operational details.

  1. Contact EOC for Fleet Cook Team activation
  2. Assess feeding requirements and location
  3. Coordinate with Walmart emergency response team
  4. Identify suitable deployment site
  5. Schedule mobile kitchen arrival and setup

Resource Requirements

  • Suitable location for mobile kitchen setup
  • Electrical power connections (high capacity)
  • Water supply access
  • Waste disposal and greywater management
  • Propane supply for cooking equipment
  • Safe vehicle access and parking area

Operational Capabilities

  • High-volume meal preparation
  • Multiple cooking stations and equipment
  • Food storage and refrigeration
  • Professional-grade kitchen facilities
  • Food safety and health code compliance
  • Dietary accommodation capabilities

Service Features

  • Trained professional cooking staff
  • Mobile kitchen fleet deployment
  • Scalable meal production capacity
  • Emergency response coordination
  • Community feeding experience
  • Corporate disaster relief partnership
