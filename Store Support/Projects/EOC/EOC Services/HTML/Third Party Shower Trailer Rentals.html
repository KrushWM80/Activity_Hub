Third Party Shower Trailer Rentals

Emergency Shower and Hygiene Services

EMERGENCY SERVICE: This service provides mobile shower facilities during emergencies when regular bathing facilities are unavailable or contaminated.

Service Overview

Third Party Shower Trailer Rentals provide essential hygiene facilities during emergency situations, natural disasters, and extended relief operations. These mobile shower units help maintain public health and personal hygiene standards for affected communities, emergency workers, and relief personnel.

Services Provided

  • Mobile shower trailer units
  • Individual shower stalls with privacy
  • Hot water heating systems
  • ADA-compliant accessible units
  • Changing areas and benches
  • Climate-controlled environment

Activation Process

  1. Contact EOC for shower trailer service activation
  2. Specify location and number of units needed
  3. Provide site access and utility requirements
  4. Coordinate delivery and setup schedule
  5. Establish operational hours and management

Resource Requirements

  • Level ground for trailer placement
  • Electrical power connection (220V/110V)
  • Water supply connection
  • Sewer or drain connection
  • Propane connection for hot water
  • Access road for trailer delivery

Facility Specifications

  • Multiple individual shower stalls per trailer
  • Hot water on-demand systems
  • Non-slip flooring and safety features
  • Ventilation and climate control
  • LED lighting for energy efficiency
  • Lockable changing areas

Operational Features

  • Gender-specific or family units available
  • ADA-compliant accessibility options
  • Professional cleaning and maintenance
  • Towel and basic hygiene supply provision
  • 24/7 operational capability
  • Security and privacy considerations
