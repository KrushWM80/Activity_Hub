WiFi Hotspot Charging Trailer

Emergency Communications and Power Services

EMERGENCY SERVICE: This service provides emergency internet connectivity and device charging capabilities during disasters when regular communications infrastructure is compromised.

Service Overview

WiFi Hotspot Charging Trailer provides critical communications infrastructure during emergency situations. These mobile units offer high-speed internet access and device charging stations to support emergency operations, public communications, and coordination efforts when regular communication services are disrupted.

Services Provided

  • High-speed WiFi internet access
  • Multiple device charging stations
  • Emergency communications support
  • Public internet access provision
  • Power generation and distribution
  • Mobile connectivity solutions
