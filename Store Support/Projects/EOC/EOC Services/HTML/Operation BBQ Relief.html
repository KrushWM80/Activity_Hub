Operation BBQ Relief

Emergency Food Service Provider

EMERGENCY SERVICE: This service provides emergency food relief during disasters and emergencies.

Service Overview

Operation BBQ Relief is a nonprofit organization that provides emergency food services during natural disasters and other crisis situations. They mobilize quickly to provide hot, fresh meals to affected communities, first responders, and relief workers.

Services Provided

  • Mobile food service units
  • Hot meal preparation and distribution
  • 24/7 emergency food service capability
  • Support for first responders and relief workers
  • Community feeding operations
  • Disaster response coordination

Activation Process

Note: Please refer to the original Word document for specific activation procedures, contact information, and operational details.

  1. Contact EOC for service activation
  2. Provide location and estimated number of people to serve
  3. Coordinate arrival time and setup requirements
  4. Establish on-site coordination point

Resource Requirements

  • Access to clean water source
  • Level ground for mobile units
  • Electrical power (if available)
  • Waste disposal arrangements
  • Traffic control and parking area
