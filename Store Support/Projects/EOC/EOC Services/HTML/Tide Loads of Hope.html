Tide Loads of Hope

Mobile Laundry Relief Program

EMERGENCY SERVICE: This service provides free mobile laundry and hygiene services to families affected by disasters and emergencies.

Service Overview

Tide Loads of Hope is P&G's mobile laundry program that provides free laundry services to families affected by natural disasters. The program operates mobile laundry units that can wash, dry, and fold clothes for disaster victims, helping restore a sense of normalcy during difficult times.

Services Provided

  • Free mobile laundry services
  • Washing, drying, and folding of clothing
  • Clean clothes distribution
  • Tide detergent and fabric care products
  • Volunteer-operated service
  • Community outreach and support

Activation Process

Note: Please refer to the original Word document for specific activation procedures, contact information, and operational details.

  1. Contact EOC to request Tide Loads of Hope activation
  2. Assess disaster impact and affected population
  3. Coordinate with Tide Loads of Hope program managers
  4. Identify suitable deployment location
  5. Schedule mobile unit arrival and setup

Resource Requirements

  • Safe, accessible location for mobile units
  • Electrical power connections
  • Water supply access
  • Waste water disposal capability
  • Parking area for mobile laundry trucks
  • Volunteer coordination support

Program Features

  • Fully equipped mobile laundry trucks
  • Professional-grade washing and drying equipment
  • Free detergent and laundry supplies
  • Trained volunteer teams
  • Community-focused service approach
  • Disaster relief partnership network

Service Capabilities

  • Multiple loads processed simultaneously
  • Quick turnaround time
  • All types of clothing and linens
  • Sanitization and cleaning expertise
  • Damage assessment and sorting
  • Distribution coordination
