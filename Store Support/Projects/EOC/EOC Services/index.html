About EOC Services

This directory provides comprehensive information about emergency services available through the Emergency Operations Center (EOC). During disasters and emergency situations, these services can be activated to support affected communities, emergency responders, and relief operations.

Important: All services must be activated through the EOC. Direct contact with service providers should only be made after EOC coordination and approval.

🍽️ Food Services

Operation BBQ Relief

Mobile food service providing hot, fresh meals during disasters. Supports affected communities, first responders, and relief workers with 24/7 emergency feeding operations.

Walmart Fleet Cook Team

Emergency mobile kitchen services with professional cooking staff. Provides large-scale meal preparation and distribution during extended emergency operations.

🚿 Hygiene & Sanitation Services

Third Party Laundry Trailers

Mobile laundry facilities with industrial-grade equipment. Provides essential clothing cleaning services for affected communities and emergency personnel.

Third Party Portable Restroom Rentals

Emergency sanitation facilities including standard and ADA-compliant portable restrooms with maintenance services and hand washing stations.

Third Party Shower Trailer Rentals

Mobile shower facilities with hot water, individual stalls, and ADA-compliant options. Maintains hygiene standards during extended emergency situations.

Tide Loads of Hope

P&G's mobile laundry program providing free washing, drying, and folding services with volunteer operation. Helps restore normalcy for disaster victims.

Walmart PG Showers

Collaborative mobile shower service between Walmart and P&G. Provides dignified bathing facilities with hygiene products during disasters.

💧 Water Services

Third Party Potable Water Truck Rentals

Emergency clean drinking water delivery service. Provides mobile water supply with quality testing and distribution equipment when regular water services fail.

📶 Communications & Technology

WiFi Hotspot Charging Trailer

Emergency communications infrastructure providing high-speed internet access and device charging stations when regular communications are compromised.

Emergency Service Activation

To activate any of these services:

  1. Contact the Emergency Operations Center (EOC)
  2. Provide detailed information about your emergency situation
  3. Specify which services are needed and location requirements
  4. EOC will coordinate with appropriate service providers
  5. Wait for EOC confirmation and service provider contact

Note: Contact information for the EOC should be obtained from your local emergency management agency. Do not contact service providers directly without EOC coordination.
