## What TrackIT Is (The "9th" Reference)

### TrackIT Definition

**Full Name:** Core Work Project Task Tracker  
**Type:** Work Management System  
**Purpose:** Track, categorize, and manage work items across all 8 projects

### TrackIT Statistics

- **Work Items Tracked:** 900+ items in FY26
- **Completion Rate:** 94.9% of planned work
- **Time Tracked:** 1,248+ hours
- **Categorization:** 
  - By Category (25 types: Development, Reporting, Maintenance, etc.)
  - By Label (Activity, Projects, Core Work, EOC, Feedback, etc.)
  - By Priority, Status, Owner
- **Features:**
  - Power Automate workflow integration
  - Automatic task cloning
  - Project promotion functionality
  - Real-time status tracking

### Why TrackIT ≠ A Project

TrackIT is a **management and reporting tool**, not a deliverable:
- ❌ Doesn't generate direct business value
- ❌ Doesn't enable users to do their jobs differently
- ❌ Doesn't create operational efficiency
- ✅ Enables internal tracking and visibility of the 8 projects

**Analogy:** Your Outlook calendar isn't a "project" even though you use it to manage projects.

- "I delivered 2 production systems and validated a $27M strategic platform"
- "I managed 8 concurrent projects across multiple business domains"
- "I tracked 900+ work items with 94.9% completion rate"

**Don't Say:**
- "I managed 9 projects" (this conflates the tracker with the work)
- "I built TrackIT" (you use it to track the 8 projects)
