## Executive Summary

Tour Guides is a strategic performance and operational enablement platform that manages the complete lifecycle of tour creation, approval, and deployment. Store leadership uses tours to systematically improve department performance, execute mandatory operational initiatives (Store Refresh, EOC), and provide clear task guidance to associates.

The platform connects tours directly to department metrics, enabling performance improvement from below-expectations to meeting/exceeding targets.

---

## Lifecycle Overview

1. **Submission** - Submitter requests tour (performance improvement, operational initiative, or training need)
2. **Review & Approval** - Stakeholder review and authorization
3. **Development** - Tour content creation and digitalization
4. **Review & Approval** - QA and final approval
5. **Output & Deployment** - Digital asset distribution to stores
6. **Data Reporting** - Usage tracking, performance metrics, and improvement validation

---

## Associate Impact (Detailed)

**Store Leadership Impact:**  
Enables store leadership to systematically review department performance and assign tours to associates for targeted improvement. Tours drive measurable department performance lift from below-expectations to meeting or exceeding targets through clear, task-based guidance.

**Operational Readiness:**  
Provides critical, actionable tours for mandatory yearly store refresh initiatives and EOC emergency response procedures, ensuring rapid, compliant execution across the enterprise.

**Metrics-Driven Improvement:**  
Tours directly connect to department-level metrics (sales, labor, shrink, customer satisfaction, etc.), enabling leadership to deploy tours as a corrective action vehicle that bridges performance gaps and sustains continuous improvement.

---

## Success Criteria (Detailed)

- **Metric Performance Lift:** Measurable improvement in department metrics for toured areas (YoY comparison: % of departments moving from "Below Expectations" to "Meeting/Exceeds"); track baseline vs. post-tour performance

- **Tour Deployment Speed:** % of approved tours deployed to stores within 30 days; time-to-value for critical initiatives (Store Refresh, EOC)

- **Adoption & Usership:** Store leadership tour assignment rate; associate tour completion/engagement rates; usage frequency across store locations

- **Time Savings & Efficiency:** Reduction in time associates spend on repetitive training; reduction in store leadership coaching time per task (tours as self-serve guidance)

- **Work Clarity & Quality:** Associate confidence scores on task execution; reduction in task rework/errors post-tour delivery; improved compliance with standard work

- **Cross-Functional Impact:** Home office and US store stakeholder satisfaction; adoption parity between home office and field operations; reduced escalations due to unclear processes

- **Enterprise Reach:** % of US stores utilizing tour platform; % of departments covered by tours; integration with existing performance management systems
