Tour Guides Platform

Enterprise-Wide Process Enablement for Store Associates

Strategic Vision

A centralized, approval-driven platform enabling Business Owners to submit process guides, build interactive tours, and publish to 4,000+ stores via MyWalmart Notes—with real-time feedback loops and performance analytics to measure departmental metric improvement.

📋 Single Intake

One submission form for all business processes—Store Refresh, EOC, performance improvement, training—eliminating duplicate systems.

⚡ Scalable Delivery

Template-based creation with cloning for recurring tours. Deploy to 4,000+ stores in one action with tracked rollout.

📊 Data-Driven

Connect tours to needed data for automation, department KPIs, and metrics that impact sales, labor, shrink, and compliance.

🔄 Feedback Loop

Live associate feedback from stores. Continuous improvement cycle with real-time store performance analytics.

4. Multi-Stage Approvals (Comms, Legal, ATC)

Same process as Activity Manager—automated notifications route to each team, built-in chat, move forward when approved.

Stage 1: Activity Manager

Reviews for completeness, business alignment. Time: 1-2 business days

Stage 2: Development Phase

Questions, data sources, Sidekick actions defined. Build demo version. Time: 3-5 business days

Stage 3: Communications Review

Tone, messaging, brand alignment. Coordinate rollout communications. Time: 1-2 business days

Stage 4: Legal Review

Compliance, policies, risk assessment. Time: 1-3 business days

Stage 5: ATC Final Review

Final approval before publishing. Time: 1 business day

Stage 6: Publish to AMP/MyWalmart Notes

Automatic push to AMP. Associates see tour in MyWalmart Notes immediately.

Total Time: Draft to Published in ~20 business days (4 weeks). Chat collaboration can compress to 2-3 weeks for urgent initiatives.

Implementation Roadmap

Phased approach to build, test, and scale.

Phase 1: Core Submission & Review (4-6 weeks)

Business Owner form, Activity Manager review, basic chat. Internal pilot with 3-5 tour requests.

Phase 2: Development Builder (4-6 weeks)

Question templating UI, data source mapping, Sidekick action integration, demo preview capability.

Phase 3: Full Approval Workflow (3-4 weeks)

Multi-stage reviews (Comms, Legal, ATC). @mention notifications. Activity Hub integration.

Phase 4: AMP & MyWalmart Notes Integration (3-4 weeks)

Automatic push to AMP. Store-level dashboard in MyWalmart Notes. Feedback loop.

Phase 5: Reporting & Analytics (2-3 weeks)

HO dashboard, tour performance metrics, department KPI correlation.

Phase 6: Cloning & Templates (1-2 weeks)

Request cloning, recurring tour support, template library.

Total Timeline: 18-25 weeks to full production. Can go live with Phase 1 (submission) in 4 weeks for early pilot validation.
