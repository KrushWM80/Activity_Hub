🔗 INTEGRATION WITH EMAIL SYSTEM
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

To connect dashboard with existing email system:

In daily_check_smart.py or your email sending logic:

  # At top of file
  from email_history_logger import log_email
  
  # After sending an email
  log_email(
      dc_number=6020,
      dc_type='Ambient',
      recipients=['6020GM@email.wal-mart.com', '6020AGM@email.wal-mart.com'],
      subject='Manager Changes - 2026-01-22',
      changes={
          'store_manager': 4,
          'market_manager': 2,
          'region_manager': 0,
          'stores_affected': 8
      },
      test_mode=config.TEST_MODE,
      status='sent'
  )

That's it! The email will be tracked and appear on the dashboard.
