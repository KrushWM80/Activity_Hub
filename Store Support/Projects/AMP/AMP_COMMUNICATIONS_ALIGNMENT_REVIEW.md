## Production Environment Standards: Rich Attachments to AMP Communications

### Goal
Empower field associates with a multi-modal communication suite that delivers critical information in the format most suited to their current environment—reducing downtime, improving store process accuracy, and bridging the engagement gap between the field and headquarters.

### AI Enhancer: Smart Modality Selection
AI continuously learns which communication formats (audio, video, PDF) perform best by role, task type, time of day, and location. Instead of sending the same message to everyone, AI ensures associates receive information in the format they are most likely to consume and act on—reducing noise while increasing effectiveness.
