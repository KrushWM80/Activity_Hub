**What Figma CANNOT do:**

- ❌ Create content at scale
- ❌ Use artificial intelligence

**Think of it this way:** Figma is like asking an artist to draw each frame of a movie by hand. It's great for creating a few design samples, but completely impractical for producing 100-150 videos per week.

## What Zorro Actually Is

**Zorro is like ChatGPT for videos** - you type what you need, and it generates the video automatically using AI. It's built specifically for automated video creation.

**What Zorro DOES:**
- ✅ Generates videos from simple text descriptions
- ✅ Automatically adds captions (required by law)
- ✅ Automatically adds audio descriptions
- ✅ Creates videos in 2 minutes instead of 8 hours
- ✅ Costs $0.10 per video instead of $500

**Think of it this way:** Zorro is like a factory assembly line - fast, consistent, and designed for high volume production.

## Legal Requirement: Accessibility

Federal law (ADA/WCAG) requires all videos to include:
- Accurate captions for deaf/hard-of-hearing associates
- Audio descriptions for blind/low-vision associates

**Figma approach:**
- Designer types captions manually → human error = compliance violations
- Hire voice actors to record audio → expensive and slow
- Compliance rate: ~60% (industry average for manual processes)
- **Legal risk: HIGH** (potential ADA lawsuits)

**Zorro approach:**
- Captions generated automatically by AI → 100% accurate
- Audio generated automatically by text-to-speech → instant
- Compliance rate: 100% (automated systems don't forget)
- **Legal risk: ZERO**
