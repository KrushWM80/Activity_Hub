Fresh Area:

Easter Week is Here! Let’s Finish Strong

Easter week is here and we need to deliver a strong finish for customers. Focus on Easter favorites: turkeys, hams, Cornish hens, boneless rib roasts and whole tenderloins. Keep bunkers full and stocked throughout the day to drive sales. Hold steady on Easter hams: The Saturday before Easter is one of the biggest sales days of the season with the average store turning the Marketside ham bunker about five times, so stock strategically. Avoid early markdowns on fresh turkeys: Merchants will monitor sell-through and support post-Easter pricing as needed, with whole fresh turkeys dropping to fifty cents per pound after Easter. Finally, protect quality with tight temperature control: Target 30 degrees; at 34 degrees, shelf life starts to drop, losing one day for each degree the temp raises. If browning or leaks are visible, check the temperatures. Review the time-lapse temperature video for examples.

Dept. 98: Fresh Bakery Easter Sell-Through, Markdowns and Inventory Management

Prep for April Fresh Cost Inventory

Fresh Cost Inventory is Tuesday, April 28. There are three tasks for salaried managers to complete. First, ensure proper staffing is in place to conduct Fresh Cost Inventory counts and provide post-inventory customer service. Second, visit the Fresh OneWalmart page. Here, you'll need to access the Ops Portal Inventory Tracking Report (ITR) to identify any potential errors, and develop a plan to resolve them. At the bottom of the page, you'll find your beginning inventory numbers (which are updated the Saturday prior to Cost Inventory). Finally, refer to the Cost Inventory section of the Fresh Better Way guide to learn more about inventory preparation, how to count, digital finalization rules for truck invoices, the finalization process, the inventory schedule and proper execution. If you have any process questions, email eomifs@email.wal-mart.com. For technical issues, submit a ticket in the MyWalmart app under Facility Management and select the Walmart Cost Inventory app.
