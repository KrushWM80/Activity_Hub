[NORMAL PACE] Easter Week is Here! Let’s Finish Strong

  --- 400ms pause ---

[NORMAL PACE] Easter week is here and we need to deliver a strong finish for customers. Focus on Easter favorites: turkeys, hams, Cornish hens, boneless rib roasts and whole tenderloins. Keep bunkers full and stocked throughout the day to drive sales. Hold steady on Easter hams: The Saturday before Easter is one of the biggest sales days of the season with the average store turning the Marketside ham bunker about five times, so stock strategically. Avoid early markdowns on fresh turkeys: Merchants will monitor sell-through and support post-Easter pricing as needed, with whole fresh turkeys dropping to fifty cents per pound after Easter. Finally, protect quality with tight temperature control: Target 30 degrees; at 34 degrees, shelf life starts to drop, losing one day for each degree the temp raises. If browning or leaks are visible, check the temperatures. Review the time-lapse temperature video for examples.
